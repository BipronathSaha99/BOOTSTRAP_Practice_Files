Bootstrap 4  Jumbotron 

A Jumbotron indicates a big grey box for calling 
extra attention to some content or information.

To use <div> element with class .jumbotron to 
create jambotron.

Full-width Jumbotron

To want a full-width jumbotron without rounded
borders, addding the .jumbotron-fluid class and a 
.container or .container-fluid inside of it.
